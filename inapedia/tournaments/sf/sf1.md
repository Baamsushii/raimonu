# Strikers Frontier #1

Strikers Frontier #1 was the first edition of the [Strikers Frontier](sfmain.md) tournament series.
18 players participated.

Tier: Minor

## Overview

### Schedule

- September 26, 2021

### Format

- Standard rules; Bo1
- Groups; Top 1 or 2 advanced to the Final Stage
- Final Stage
  - Single Elimination
