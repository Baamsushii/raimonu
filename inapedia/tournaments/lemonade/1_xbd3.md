# XBackdraft #3 Goujin

XBackdraft #3 Goujin was the third edition of the [Lemonade XBackdraft](bdmain.md) tournament series for its premier Goujin league.
8 players participated.

Tier: National :brazil:

## Overview

### Schedule

### Format
- Group of 8
    - Single Round Robin 
    - Top 3 advances to Final Stage
- Final Stage
    - Single Elimination
