# Ragnarok Strikers Xtreme #3

Ragnarok Strikers Xtreme #3 was the 3rd edition of the [Ragnarok Strikers Xtreme](ragnaxmain.md) tournament series.
36 players participated.

Tier: Minor

## Overview

### Format
- Standard rules
- Double Elimination
- All matches are Bo1
