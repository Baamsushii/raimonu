# Ragnarok Strikers Xtreme #4

Ragnarok Strikers Xtreme #4 was the 4th edition of the [Ragnarok Strikers Xtreme](ragnaxmain.md) tournament series.
46 players participated.

Tier: Major

## Overview

### Schedule
- April 22nd, 2023 to May 4th, 2023

### Format
- Standard rules
- Double Elimination
- All matches are Bo1
