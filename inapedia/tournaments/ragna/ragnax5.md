# Ragnarok Strikers Xtreme #5

Ragnarok Strikers Xtreme #5 was the 5th edition of the [Ragnarok Strikers Xtreme](ragnaxmain.md) tournament series.
54 players participated.

Tier: Major

## Overview

### Schedule
- June 3rd, 2023 to June 11th, 2023

### Format
- Standard rules
- Double Elimination
- All matches are Bo1
