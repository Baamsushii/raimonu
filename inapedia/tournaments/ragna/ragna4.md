# Ragnarok Strikers #4

Ragnarok Strikers #4 was the third edition of the [Ragnarok Strikers](ragnamain.md) tournament series.
64 players participated.

Tier: Major

## Overview

### Schedule
- June 4th, 2022 to June 13th, 2022

### Format
- Standard rules
- Double Elimination
- All matches are Bo1
