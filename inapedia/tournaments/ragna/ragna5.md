# Ragnarok Strikers #5

Ragnarok Strikers #5 was the fifth edition of the [Ragnarok Strikers](ragnamain.md) tournament series.
51 players participated.

Tier: Major

## Overview

### Schedule
- July 16th, 2022 to July 24th, 2022

### Format
- Standard rules
- Double Elimination
- All matches are Bo1
