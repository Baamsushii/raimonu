# Zenith

Zenith was a tournament held in the IE GO Strikers 2013 discord server.
60 players participated.

Tier: Major

## Overview

### Schedule
- 30 December, 2023 to 6 January, 2024

### Format
- Standard rules
- Groups of 5; Top 2 advances to Final Stage
  - Double Elimination
  - Bo1 
- Final Stage
  - Double Elimination
  - Bo1
